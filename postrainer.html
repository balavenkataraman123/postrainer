About Late Night Drives!

"Our mission is to combat and alleviate mental health illnesses in the youth community for those who don't have access to safe resources."

- With Love From the Late Night Drive Team🌌

We as a team aim to provide the youth who havelimited access to important mental health resources with a safe platform to both educate and supply the support they need. Late Night Drives is dedicated to highlighting the younger age group, who tend to be overlooked when discussing/trying to find help with their mental illnesses. With our platform, we strive to advocate for a higher demand in youth mental health services while making information in the community much more accessible.
